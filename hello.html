Zo Computer: Obsidian: Hello, Obsidian

Your notes stay as normal Markdown files. The browser is just the window into your vault. You run the Obsidian web renderer on your own Zo.

This is not remote desktop. No Electron needed. Instead, obsidian-web loads Obsidian's original renderer (app.js) unmodified and replaces every Node.js / Electron dependency with lightweight HTTP shims.

What you need: a Zo account, a copy of your vault, the obsidian-web project, and a private Zo route or service to host it.
